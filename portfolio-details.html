From College Projects to Freelancing: My Journey as a Self-Taught Developer

Every developer has a unique story, and mine is a mix of working full-time and pursuing freelance projects driven by pure passion. While completing my B.Tech in Computer Engineering, I discovered my interest in web development and started building small projects for fun. That spark gradually turned into freelance gigs on platforms like LinkedIn and through personal contacts.

Along the way, I’ve worked on several exciting freelance projects alongside my job. One of my most fulfilling projects was Panchastra – Revolutionizing Real Estate, built using React, TypeScript, Firebase, and Tailwind CSS, where I contributed as a Full-Stack Developer.
